Chicken Florentine Recipe

Chicken Florentine

This tasty quick spin on chicken Florentine is colorful and exciting. Serve together with your choice of rice, potato, or couscous, my favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 medium shallot, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Directions

Step 1 - Season chicken with salt and pepper.

Step 2 - Heat oil in a deep skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and saute until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 6 to 8 minutes. Remove chicken to a plate.
