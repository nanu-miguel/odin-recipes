Karaage Recipe

Karaage

Learn two methods of frying that produce moist, flavorful, boneless, skinless chicken with a super-thin coating that's incredibly crispy and crunchy. Serve with lemon or lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic, finely grated
  • 1 tablespoon finely grated fresh ginger
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons sake
  • 1 tablespoon mirin

Directions

Step 1 - Mix garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sake, mirin, sesame oil, black pepper, cayenne, sugar, and salt in a medium bowl.

Step 2 - Place chicken in the marinade and mix until thoroughly and evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for 1 to 10 hours.
